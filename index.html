ABOUT CURRENT CUT FILM FEST

Current Cut Film Festival is an opportunity for students to take centre stage, showcasing their bold and visionary work. We invite you to pull up a Muskoka chair and experience firsthand the passion and creativity of tomorrow’s filmmakers. This festival is for those who defy expectations, challenge the norm, and venture into uncharted creative waters. It’s a celebration of the daring storytellers. The filmmakers who don’t just ride the waves of cinema but carve new currents. Like the lake that borders our campus, each film screened at CCFF brings something fresh, original, and thought-provoking to the world. Be among the first to witness this rising talent, all while soaking in the breathtaking lakeside setting and the inspiring atmosphere of Humber Polytechnic’s Lakeshore campus. At Current Cut Film Festival, you’re not just an observer. You’re part of something extraordinary.
